          <h1>
<span>What’s Your Dosha?</span>
</h1>
          <h2>
  <div class="field field--name-field-gp-subtitle field--type-string field--label-hidden field__items">
        <div class="field__item">Take the quiz!</div>
  </div></h2>

      <section class="inner-wrapper main-content">
        <div class="span-9 center">
                    
  <div class="text-content clearfix field field--name-field-gp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden field__items">
        <div class="field__item"><p>The ancient health science of <strong><a href="https://kripalu.org/resources/what-ayurveda-anyway">Ayurveda</a></strong> is built around the five elements of ether, air, fire, water, and earth. The unique combination of these elements come together to make up the <strong><a href="https://kripalu.org/resources/ayurvedic-doshas">doshas</a></strong>, or constitutions—vata, pitta, and kapha. Each dosha has a number of corresponding qualities that are expressed in the physical, emotional, and mental aspects of one’s being. All of us have some aspects of each dosha, but one or two doshas tend to dominate, informing everything from our <strong><a href="https://kripalu.org/resources/ayurvedic-wisdom-better-digestion">digestion</a></strong> to our <strong><a href="https://kripalu.org/resources/ayurvedic-eating-tips-balance-energy-and-emotions">emotions</a></strong>.</p>

<p>Take this quiz to discover your dosha, and then explore how it affects your everyday life!</p>

<form action="" id="doshaquiz" method="post" name="doshaquiz">
<div class="row">
<div class="col-xs-12 col-md-6 b20"><strong>1. Which of these best describes your physical frame?</strong><br>
<label><input form="doshaquiz" id="vata1" name="question1" onclick="getTotal()" type="radio" value="1"> Small</label><br>
<label><input form="doshaquiz" id="pitta1" name="question1" onclick="getTotal()" type="radio" value="1"> Medium</label><br>
